Banana Pancakes

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 3 adults
Ingredients Quantity
banana 3
oats 4 heaped handfulls
eggs 3
greek yoghurt 100 gramms
butter a bit

Step 1: Peel the bananas and place them in a bowl.
Step 2: Mash the bananas with fork.
Step 3:Add in the oats, greek yoghurt and eggs. Mix them.
Step 4: Heat up your pan and melt some butter onto it.
Step 5: Use a spoon to place the batter onto the pan. Use abotu two spponfuls per pancake.
Step 6: Fry until bubbles form on the surface and then flip the pancakes. Fry until the second side is also golden.
Step 7: Serve and enjoy!

Pita Bread

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
water 50 ml
flour 80 gramms
salt a pinch

Step 1: Mix all the ingredients together until you get a nice, round ball of dough. It shouldn’t stick, but it should be soft. Divide the dough into tiny balls, according to how large you want the pitas.
Step 2: Put a pan on the stove and get it hot first, Preferably use a non- stick pan, but I don't think it matters much.
Step 3:Using a rolling pin, roll out the ball dough.
Step 4:Place the rolled out pita on the hot pan and let it bake for a couple of minutes on each side.
Step 5: Once the pita bread baked on one side there will be tiny bubbles forming on the surface. Flip it over and bake it on the other side for 30 sec or so.
Step 6: If you want it crispy don't cover it with the lid of the pan. If you want it soft, right after it’s baked, place the hot pita bread in a fresh kitchen towel and cover it; the steam will soften them and they will be flexible.
Step 7: Serve and enjoy!
